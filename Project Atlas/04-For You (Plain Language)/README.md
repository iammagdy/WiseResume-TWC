This folder is written for **the owner**, not for engineers or AI agents. No code. No file paths. No technical acronyms unless explained right here.

It mirrors the structure of the other three sections of the Atlas:

- "Current features" — what's built today.
- "Coming soon" — what's planned next.
- "Under discussion" — what's just an idea right now.
- "Glossary" — every Wise-specific word, in everyday English.

---

## How to use this folder

1. Open **Current features** to see what the app can do **today** for both job seekers and HR companies.
2. Open **Coming soon** to see what your team has agreed to build **next**.
3. Open **Under discussion** to see ideas that are just being talked about — nothing has been promised yet.
4. If a word in any doc is confusing, check the **Glossary**.

If you ever want to dig deeper into the "how" behind any topic, the engineering version lives in the matching section of this Atlas one level up — but you don't need to.

---

## What the platform is, in two sentences

**The Wise Cloud** is your AI-powered career platform. It runs two products from one codebase: **WiseResume** for job seekers (build resumes, get AI feedback, prep interviews, host a public portfolio, track applications) and **WiseHire** for HR teams and recruiters (write job descriptions, screen candidates, run a hiring pipeline) — both sharing the same login, database, and admin panel.
