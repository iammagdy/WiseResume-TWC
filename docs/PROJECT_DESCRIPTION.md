# WiseResume — Project Description

> **Your AI Career Companion**

---

## Elevator Pitch

WiseResume is an AI-powered career platform that helps job seekers build ATS-optimized resumes, practice interviews with a voice-based AI coach, and get brutally honest feedback from simulated recruiters — all from their phone. It turns the stressful job search into a guided, confidence-building experience.

---

## The Problem

Job seekers today face three core frustrations:

1. **"Is my resume good enough?"** — Most people have no way to know if their resume will pass ATS filters or impress a recruiter. They apply to hundreds of jobs and hear nothing back.
2. **"I don't know how to tailor my resume."** — Every job posting is different, but rewriting a resume for each one takes hours. Most people just send the same generic version everywhere.
3. **"I'm terrified of interviews."** — There's no safe, private way to practice answering tough questions out loud. Friends and family can't simulate a real recruiter's mindset.

---

## The Solution

WiseResume solves each pain point with purpose-built AI:

| Pain Point | WiseResume Solution |
|---|---|
| "Is it good enough?" | **Instant AI scoring** — get a 0–100 match score against any job in seconds |
| "I can't tailor it" | **One-click tailoring** — AI rewrites your entire resume for a specific role, adding metrics and keywords |
| "I'm scared of interviews" | **Voice-based mock interviews** — talk to an AI interviewer who scores every answer using the STAR method |

---

## Key Features

### 🎯 Smart Resume Builder
Build a professional resume in minutes with 12 polished templates optimized for ATS compatibility. Supports 13 resume sections including experience, education, skills, certifications, projects, publications, volunteering, awards, hobbies, and references.

### ⚡ AI Resume Tailoring ("Supercharged Engine")
Paste any job description and WiseResume rewrites your entire resume — summary, skills, experience bullets — optimized for that specific role. Turns generic bullet points into metric-driven achievements:

> **Before:** "Worked on frontend development"  
> **After:** "Architected and shipped 15+ React components serving 50K+ daily users, reducing page load time by 40%"

### 🎙️ Mock Interview Coach
Practice interviews by speaking out loud. The AI interviewer asks role-specific questions, listens to your answers in real-time, and scores each response on a 1–10 scale with improvement tips. Supports both general and job-targeted interview modes.

### 👥 Recruiter Simulator
Get feedback from 4 distinct AI recruiter personas — each with a different hiring philosophy:

| Persona | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Sarah Chen** (Fortune 500) | Structure, career progression, brand names |
| **Marcus Rivera** (Startup) | Scrappiness, ownership, real impact |
| **Priya Sharma** (Tech/FAANG) | Technical depth, scale, system design |
| **James O'Connor** (Executive) | Career narrative, P&L ownership, board presence |

Each gives a hireability score, red flags, interview questions they'd ask, and a verdict: **Would Call / Maybe / Pass**.

### 📝 Cover Letter & Resignation Letter Generator
Generate tailored cover letters in three tones (Professional, Enthusiastic, Conversational). Also create resignation letters with customizable notice periods and a departure checklist.

### 📋 Job Application Tracker
Track every application on a Kanban-style board with statuses, deadlines, reminders, and linked resumes/cover letters.

### 🌐 Public Portfolio
Generate a shareable online portfolio from your resume with a custom username, theme, and analytics (visitor count, sections viewed, referral sources).

### 🧭 Career Path Advisor
Take a career assessment quiz and receive AI-generated career path recommendations with milestone tracking and skills gap analysis.

## AI Intelligence

WiseResume uses a multi-model AI architecture, automatically selecting the best AI model for each task:

- **Deep reasoning tasks** (resume tailoring) → Most capable model for thorough analysis
- **Fast tasks** (scoring, chat, parsing) → Speed-optimized models for instant results
- **Creative tasks** (headshot generation) → Image-specialized models

Users can also bring their own API keys (BYOK) for unlimited usage.
